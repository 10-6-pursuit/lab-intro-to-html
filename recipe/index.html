Pancake Recipe

Link to original recipe

Description

Perfect recipe for classic pancakes, just like your parents used to make

Ingredients

  • 250g plain flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 250 ml buttermilk

Directions

  1. Sift the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Make a hole in the middle and add the egg.
  3. Mix them quickly after breaking the yolk and pouring in the buttermilk unitl they become stick batter
  4. Do not beat when mixing as gluten will appear in the flour, which will prevent the pancakes from rising
  5. Fry the mixture on a hot griddle pan and serve hot
